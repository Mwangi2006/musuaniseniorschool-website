About Musuani Secondary School

Musuani Secondary School is a public secondary institution domiciled in Kenya and governed under the Basic Education Act, 2013. The school is committed to providing quality education that nurtures academic excellence, discipline, and holistic learner development.

School History

Musuani Secondary School is located in Kitui County, Migwani Sub-County, Thokoa Zone. The school was officially registered on 14th March 2006 under registration number 138300012248.

The school is categorized as an Extra County Public school (National, Extra County, County, and Sub-County) established, owned, and operated by the Ministry of Education (Kenya).

Musuani Secondary School operates as a day and boarding school. As at 30th June 2025, the school had a student population of 300 learners, organized into two double streams. The school has 20 teachers, all deployed by the Teachers Service Commission (TSC), with none employed directly by the School Board of Management.
