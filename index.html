Our Curriculum

Our school implements the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) as approved by the Ministry of Education. The curriculum is designed to equip learners with practical skills, values, and competencies required for academic success and lifelong learning.

We offer Senior Secondary curriculum under CBE system, with a strong focus on academic achievement, talent development, and career preparedness.

Transition to the Competency-Based Education (CBE)

As Kenya embraces the Competency-Based Curriculum, Musuani Secondary School is actively preparing to align our teaching approaches and facilities with this new system. We believe in equipping students with future-ready skills while maintaining our tradition of academic excellence. As the first batch of Grade 10 joins us in 2026, we are excited to welcome them.
