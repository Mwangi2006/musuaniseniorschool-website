Admissions

Welcome to our school! We accept applications for both CBE (Senior Secondary) and 8-4-4 (Senior Secondary) streams. Please find below all details about eligibility, required documents, admission process, deadlines, and fees. Use the buttons below to view the stream you want to apply for.

CBE – Senior Secondary Admissions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of Primary School (Grade 6 or equivalent)
  • Age within Ministry of Education guidelines
  • Readiness for Senior Secondary learning

Required Documents

  • Completed Admission Form
  • Birth Certificate / National ID
  • KCPE / Primary School Report Card
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Medical / Vaccination Records

Admission Process

  • Fill the application form (online or at school)
  • Submit required documents to the Admissions Office
  • Attend assessment or interview if applicable
  • Receive confirmation and fee details
  • Complete fee payment to secure placement

Application Deadlines

  • Term 1: Applications close by January 2026
  • Term 2: Applications close by May 2026
  • Term 3: Applications close by September 2026
