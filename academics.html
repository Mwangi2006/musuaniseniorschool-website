Academics

Our academic programs are structured to deliver holistic education through KICD approved curricula, ensuring learners are equipped with knowledge, skills, values, and competencies for future success.

Competency Based Education (CBE)

The Senior School CBE program focuses on nurturing learners' competencies, creativity, values, and real-world problem-solving skills. Learning is learner-centered, practical, and inquiry-based.

Our Competency Based Education (CBE) program is designed to nurture learners who are confident, creative, and capable of applying knowledge to real-life situations. Learning goes beyond textbooks to focus on skills, values, and personal growth.

Through project-based learning, collaborative activities, and continuous assessment, learners develop problem-solving skills, communication abilities, digital literacy, and responsible citizenship from an early age.

The school provides a supportive Senior School environment with trained teachers, modern learning resources, and structured mentorship to ensure a smooth transition from Primary to Senior School education under the CBE system.

Admissions are open for Senior School (Grade10 – 12). Parents and guardians are encouraged to apply early.

8-4-4 Curriculum (KCSE)

The KCSE program emphasizes academic excellence, discipline, and national examination preparedness. Learners are guided through a rigorous syllabus aligned with the Kenya National Examinations Council.

The school has a strong and consistent history of offering the 8-4-4 curriculum, with a proven record of academic excellence and disciplined learning. Our KCSE program is built on firm academic foundations, structured syllabus coverage, and rigorous examination preparation.

Over the years, our candidates have demonstrated steady improvement in national examinations, supported by experienced teachers, targeted revision programs, academic counseling, and regular performance tracking.

The school has successfully presented KCSE candidates for several years, producing graduates who progress to universities, colleges, and other tertiary institutions across the country.
